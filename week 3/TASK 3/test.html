Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Custor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
Self raising Flour
A pre-mixed of flour and leveling agents (usually salt and baking powder)

Method

  1. Preheat teh oven to 190°C
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl , whip togeather the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20minutes in the preheated oven , or until the top of cake springs back when lightly pressed
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
